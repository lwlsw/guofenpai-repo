iOS 11 users: Differences between iOS 11 and iOS 12 has resulted in some functions not being fully supported on iOS 11 such as animations for Apple Watches and grouped AirPods.

Banner Support:

Charging banners support most connected devices, including AirPods (Gen 1) for dual device banners.
Banner sizing can be configured via settings.
