A feature-rich control center for iOS.

Features:

Modules:

Prysm includes several modules that can be removed and reordered from the settings page.
Available modules include:

  1. Connectivity toggles.
  2. Brightness and Audio sliders.
  3. Quick access toggles.
  4. Media widget.
  5. Weather conditions.
  6. Power controls (reboot, shutdown, respring, safemode and lock).
  7. Bluetooth Devices

The quick access toggle module responds to your existing control center configuration. It supports all small toggle modules. Any modules not placed in the quick access module can be found by swiping up on the Prysm control center.

Appearance Modes:

Prysm supports two appearance modes, light and dark. Integrating with the iOS 13 dark mode and Noctis12, NoctisXI for a seamless dark mode transition.

Haptic Touch:

Most Prysm modules support Haptic Touch. Simply long-press on an element to reveal the expanded content. Haptic Touch is also supported on most toggle modules activated through the iOS control center configuration.

Gestures:

Prysm provides a new means of accessing the control center. Simply swipe up from the bottom corners of your device, or enable the stock gesture to access Prysm like the regular control center.

Status Widgets:

Easily see the most important information using status widgets. An indicator cluster (iOS 13+) and battery widget are placed above the Prysm card and update instantly.

Tweak Support:

Prysm supports most control center tweaks right out of the box. All additional toggle modules can be displayed within the quick access module or hidden in the toggle cluster under the control center.
Community favorite Magma Evo seamlessly integrates with Prysm to offer nearly limitless customization potential. Check out Magma Evo here.
Prysm supports all jailbroken devices running iOS 11 or later.
