Introduction

Weather13 is XenHTML widget made to be modular and well designed.
The widget allows you to have the weather and even a clock, or maybe you want the temperature, sure Weather13 can do that too, or maybe you want the chance of rain? Yup it does that too, maybe the weather and chance of rain, also possible!

Feature List

  • Place the widget anywhere on any page, or even on your lockscreen thanks to XenHTML
  • Hold down your finger on the widget to reload the weather or the widget itself
  • Choose shadow from pre-made ones or make your own
  • Choose from light or dark theme
  • Customize the widget as much as you want, you can mix the clock and temperature together, or even mix weather and temperature and so forth.
  • Change which side has what
  • Change from light to dark mode
  • Change between 12hr and 24hr clocks
  • Change the date format
  • Change reload speed to your liking, either updating quicker or prioritizing battery (Fast, Fastest and Perfomance)
  • Change the padding on the sides
