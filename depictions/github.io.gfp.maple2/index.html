Maple 2
Tweaks

Charging animations and banners for iOS 13.

On iOS 12 or lower? Get Maple: https://repo.packix.com/package/com.laughingquoll.maple
Maple brings device specific charging animations to your iOSd device. Maple 2 is the successor to Maple and now features customisable iPhone and Watch models as well as support for multiple new devices.

Banner Support:

Charging banners support most connected devices, including AirPods and AirPods Pro for dual device banners.
Banner sizing can be configured via settings.
